Who we are

Led by technology powered by creative thinking & driven by innovative partnerships

DMI Finance is reimagining the transmission of credit in India.

Founded in 2008 by Shivashish Chatterjee and Yuvraja C. Singh, DMI Finance is a systemically important non-banking financial company and part of the DMI Group.

Our vision

To be the leading long-term credit business in new India - using technology,
innovation, and strategic partnerships to democratise access to credit.

Values that define us

Strengthening communities, changing lives

Empowerment
